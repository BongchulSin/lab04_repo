Problem

Problem Area: The problem area we chose is local transportation in the Greater Vancouver Area, but specifically commuting for work/school/events. The 2 main opportunties are cost and time. We felt that with our solution a carpool app there is lots of room to save on cost and save time while commuting. With car pooling the cost of owning a car (insurance, gas, wear and tear, parking) drop since the cost is split between 2 or more people. Time can be saved because carpooling lets drivers take the HOV lane which has significantly less traffic than regular lanes.
